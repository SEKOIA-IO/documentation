## Overview

Trapster deploys honeypots on your network to detect lateral movements, malicious scans and insider threats. Few false positives, instant alerts, and reduction of your detection time.

- **Vendor**: Ballpoint
- **Supported environment**: On prem
- **Detection based on**: Alert
- **Supported application or feature**: Honeypot alerts (lateral movement, scans, threats)

For setup and raw event samples, see the [Trapster integration page](/integration/categories/network_security/trapster.md).
