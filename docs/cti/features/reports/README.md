# Reports

Use the **Reports** module to access and analyze threat intelligence reports in the Intelligence database. These reports provide structured, contextualized information about threats, actors, campaigns, and infrastructure.

Reports are divided into two categories:

* [**FLINTs**](flints.md): analyst‑driven, in‑depth investigations and strategic/technical reports produced by Sekoia’s Threat Detection & Research team.
* [**External reports**](external-reports.md): reports published by other organizations which have been parsed by our teams to extract intelligence objects and indicators usable in the platform.

All reports are linked to observables, threat actors, malware, and other intelligence objects so you can pivot from a report to related entities and vice versa.
