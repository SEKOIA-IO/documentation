# FLINTs

**FLINTs** are Flash Threat Intelligence reports written by Sekoia’s Threat Detection & Research team. They provide analyst‑led investigations, contextual analysis, and actionable insights into current threats, campaigns, and attacker behavior.

FLINTs include:

* Exclusive investigations not available in open‑source feeds.
* Technical and strategic analysis of threat actors, infrastructure, and campaigns.
* Timely updates on emerging or evolving threats.
* Intelligence objects (indicators, actors, malware, etc.) directly linked to the report.

By design, FLINTs are:

* **Analyst‑driven**: written and reviewed by Sekoia’s internal analysts.
* **Contextualized**: focused on relevance, not just raw data.
* **Actionable**: mapped to observables and intelligence that can be used in detection, hunting, and enrichment workflows.

Depending on your subscription, FLINTs may be identified as **paid** or **exclusive** content in the platform.
