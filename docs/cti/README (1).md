# Threat Context (Intelligence)

The Intelligence Module gives you access to **up-to-date cyber threat intelligence** that is contextualized, analyst-reviewed, and continuously maintained by Sekoia’s **Threat Detection & Research team.**

Cyber Threat Intelligence is central to cybersecurity operations. The module helps you focus on relevant intelligence, not just large volumes of indicators. Every item is framed with explicit validity timestamps and is continuously updated, so you can rely on information that is current and actionable.

Through **our publications** and access to the [intelligence database](features/intelligence.md), you can follow cyber threat activity as it unfolds. Our analysts process, cross-analyze, and contextualize information from more than 500 sources to track over 200 attacker groups and their infrastructure worldwide.

Unlike simple indicator feeds, the module focuses on **relevance**, **validity**, and **threat context** so you can monitor threats, investigate activity, and understand attacker infrastructure while it is still useful.

### What you can do <a href="#what-you-can-do" id="what-you-can-do"></a>

#### Consume and investigate <a href="#consume-and-investigate" id="consume-and-investigate"></a>

Browse cyber threat intelligence through a searchable database and analyst publications. Use the content to understand attackers, infrastructure, campaigns, and related indicators.

#### Stay up to date <a href="#stay-up-to-date" id="stay-up-to-date"></a>

Follow threat activity through continuously updated intelligence and publications. Get the latest information while it is still relevant.

#### Integrate with the API <a href="#integrate-with-the-api" id="integrate-with-the-api"></a>

Clients can connect the Intelligence Module to their own tools, playbooks, and workflows via the Sekoia API. Use the API to pull structured intelligence, indicators, and context into your own platforms and automate consumption at scale.

#### Report and monitor <a href="#report-and-monitor" id="report-and-monitor"></a>

Use dashboards and widgets to track intelligence production, coverage, and threat landscape evolution. Build views that match your reporting needs.

#### Analyst coverage <a href="#analyst-coverage" id="analyst-coverage"></a>

The Intelligence Module is supported by Sekoia’s Threat Detection & Research team. Their work includes research, analysis, and publications that turn raw information into actionable intelligence.

This includes FLINT reports and other analyst content that help you understand threats with more context and better timing.

### Why it matters <a href="#why-it-matters" id="why-it-matters"></a>

Many threat intel tools focus on indicator volume. The Intelligence Module focuses on intelligence that is current, contextualized, and useful for operational security work.

That means you can move from raw signals to informed decisions faster, with better visibility into threats, attacker behavior, and active infrastructure.

### In short <a href="#in-short" id="in-short"></a>

The Intelligence Module is the entry point to Sekoia’s cyber threat intelligence content. It combines analyst expertise, a continuously updated database, contextualized publications, and a robust API so you can stay informed, integrate intelligence into your tooling, and investigate threats effectively.
