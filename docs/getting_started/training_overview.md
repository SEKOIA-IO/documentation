# Training offer overview

Sekoia.io provides a structured training program designed to help security analysts, IT administrators, and MSSPs build proficiency with the platform. The program is organized into three levels, from foundational concepts to advanced certification.

## Course catalog

| Course | Level | Delivery | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| 101 Sekoia.io Fundamentals | Beginner | Online self-paced | 3 to 4 hours |
| 201 Initiation to the Custom Formats | Intermediate | Online self-paced | 3 to 4 hours |
| 202 Initiation to the Playbooks | Intermediate | Online self-paced | 3 to 4 hours |
| 301 Certified Sekoia.io Security Analyst | Advanced | Instructor-led remote | 1 day (two half-days) |
| 302 Certified Sekoia.io Integrator | Advanced | Instructor-led remote | 1 day (two half-days) |

## Prerequisites

The 301 and 302 certifications require prior completion of the following courses:

- 101 Sekoia.io Fundamentals
- 201 Initiation to the Custom Formats (exam)
- 202 Initiation to the Playbooks (exam)

## Certification exam

The 301 and 302 certifications are validated by a Multiple Choice Question (MCQ) exam. Key details:

| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| Format | Remote MCQ, taken from your own workstation |
| Duration | 1 hour |
| Deadline | Must be completed within the week following the training day |
| Passing score | 80% |
| Retake | One retake allowed if the score is below 80% |
| Validity | 2 years |
