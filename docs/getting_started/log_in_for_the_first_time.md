# Log in for the first time

When your administrator invites you to a Sekoia workspace, you receive an email with a link to set your password and access the platform. This article walks you through the process from invitation to your first successful login.

## Before you begin

Check that you have:

- Received a Sekoia invitation email from `no-reply@sekoia.io`
- Access to the email address the invitation was sent to

!!! note "Did not receive the invitation?"
    Check your spam or junk folder. If the email is not there, ask your workspace administrator to resend the invitation from **Settings > Users**.

## Step 1: Accept the invitation

1. Open the invitation email.
2. Click the **Set your password** link.

The link is valid for 24 hours. If it has expired, ask your administrator to resend the invitation.

## Step 2: Set your password

1. Enter a password in the **Password** field.
2. Enter the same password in the **Confirm password** field.
3. Click **Set password**.

## Step 3: Identify your region URL

Sekoia is available across multiple regions. The URL you use to access the platform depends on the region where your workspace is hosted.

!!! warning "Use the correct URL"
    If you use a region URL that does not match your workspace, you will not be able to log in. Ask your administrator which region your workspace uses.

| Region | URL |
|---|---|
| France (FRA1) | `https://app.sekoia.io` |
| France SecNumCloud (FRA2) | `https://app.fra2.sekoia.io` |
| Monaco (MCO1) | `https://app.mco1.sekoia.io` |
| United Arab Emirates (UAE1) | `https://app.uae1.sekoia.io` |
| United States (USA1) | `https://app.usa1.sekoia.io` |
| Singapore (SGP1) | `https://app.sgp1.sekoia.io` |

## Step 4: Log in

1. Navigate to your region URL in your browser.
2. Enter your email address and password.
3. Click **Log in**.

If your workspace has two-factor authentication (2FA) required or if you have already enabled it on your account, you will be prompted to enter your verification code at this step.

## Step 5: Complete your profile

On your first login, review your profile settings:

1. Click your name at the bottom of the navigation menu.
2. Select **Profile**.
3. Confirm your display name and contact information.

## Troubleshooting login issues

| Problem | What to do |
|---|---|
| Invitation link expired | Ask your administrator to resend the invitation from **Settings > Users** |
| Forgotten password | Click **Forgot password** on the login page and enter your email address |
| Cannot reach the login page | Verify you are using the correct region URL (see Step 3 above) |
| 2FA code not accepted | Check that the time on your device is synchronized; if needed, ask your administrator to reset your 2FA |
