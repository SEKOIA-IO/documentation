# Configure custom verdicts

Custom verdicts enable SOC teams to standardize classifications of alerts and cases beyond simple true or false positive determinations.

## Create a custom verdict

1. On the left panel, navigate to **Settings** > **Configuration** > **Custom Statuses** > **Analyst verdict**.
2. Click **+** to create a new verdict in the appropriate category (**True Positive** or **False Positive**).
3. Enter a **Name** (e.g., "Policy Violation").
4. Enter a **Description** about when the verdict should be used.
5. Click **Save**.


## Manage verdicts

- **Edit** — Click the verdict to update its details and click **Save**.
- **Delete** — A verdict cannot be deleted if it has been applied to an alert or case. You must first reassign those items before deleting the verdict from the configuration page.
