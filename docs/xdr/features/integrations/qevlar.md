# Qevlar

## What is Qevlar?

[Qevlar AI](https://www.qevlar.com/) offers an autonomous SOC investigation platform leveraging graph-based AI to contextualize alerts, reduce false positives, and automate security investigations, significantly improving analyst productivity and response speed.

By correlating complex data sets, Qevlar AI helps security teams prioritize real threats and streamline incident response workflows.

The solution is designed for SOC teams facing high alert volumes.

## How does Qevlar work with Sekoia XDR?

Qevlar integrates with Sekoia XDR (Extended Detection and Response) to receive detections and to access the platform's threat intelligence context during investigations.

The combination is particularly relevant for European organizations that benefit from Sekoia's CTI coverage of regional threat actors, as Qevlar can use that intelligence to enrich and contextualize the findings it surfaces automatically.

Please consult [the official Qevlar documentation](https://help.qevlar.com/hc/en-001/articles/36824774679954-Sekoia) to connect Qevlar to Sekoia XDR and investigate detections.
